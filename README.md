## Section 5 - Meet the Team

Layout: Horizontal scrolling carousel of cards. Each card = portrait photo + name (bold) + bio text. Arrow navigation on the right side.

### Team Members

#### Dana, Owner
Dana's passion for dogs inspired her to pursue a career in professional grooming. She graduated from Animal Haven Dog Grooming School in 2017, where she trained in precision grooming and hand stripping under two master groomers. After gaining experience in a busy Vancouver salon, she joined Suds 'n' Scissors in 2022 and proudly became the owner in 2025. Dana's approach to grooming is rooted in understanding each dog's behavior, temperament, and individual needs. Every grooming session is personalized to build trust, reduce stress, and deliver beautiful, lasting results.

#### Mana
Mana is a graduate of Tokyo Communication Arts College in Japan. For 3 years, she learned to be a dog groomer, dog trainer and vet assistant, graduating in 2014. She then studied dog training in Australia for three months. Mana moved to Vancouver in 2014 and joined Suds 'n' Scissors in Summer 2017. With slow steady hands and tons of patience, Mana has a special way to calm the fussiest and most nervous dogs.

#### Rachel
Rachel has over 10 years of experience in pet grooming, having trained at Western Dog Grooming School (2014) and Kim's Dog Grooming School in Seoul, Korea (2017).

#### Nari Lee - Asian Style Pet Stylist
Nari completed grooming training in Korea and is Fear Free Certified. She specialises in low-stress Asian Fusion styling.

## Visual Design Reference

### Colour Palette

| Name          | Hex (approx.) | Usage                          |
|---------------|---------------|--------------------------------|
| Warm Cream    | #F2EDE4       | Primary page background        |
| Warm Sand/Tan | #D4C5AE       | Section alternate background   |
| Dark Charcoal | #1E2D2A       | Text, headings, buttons        |
| Soft Taupe    | #C8B89A       | Card surfaces, dividers        |

### Typography
- Display / Headings: Heavy serif italic (similar to Playfair Display or Canela) - hero headline and section headings
- Body: Clean sans-serif - body paragraphs, lists, captions
- Nav: Small, light weight sans-serif, all-caps on CTA button

### Layout Patterns
- Split 50/50 hero with photo left, text right
- Circular cropped photos on service cards
- Horizontal scrolling team carousel
- 4-column Instagram photo grid
- Two-column contact + form layout
- Colour blocking used for section separation (no SVG dividers)

### Photography Style
- Professional pet photography with dogs as centre-subjects
- Studio-style grooming shots (dark or neutral backgrounds)
- Warm, natural lifestyle shots for team
- Tilted/editorial framing used for some images (A-La-Carte section)
