## Services Section
- **Heading:** Our Services
- **Subtitle:** Personalized care packages tailored to your dog's breed, coat condition, and temperament.
- **Service Options:**
  - **Full Grooming** (From $92 · Breed & style dependent)
    - Full bath with premium, breed-appropriate shampoo
    - Blow dry and thorough brush-out
    - Professional haircut or custom Asian Fusion styling
    - Nail trimming and filing
    - Paw pad, sanitary area shave, and ear cleaning/plucking

  - **Bath and Tidy** (From $85 · Medium–Long coat breeds)

    - Trimming around the eyes and paws

    - Ear cleaning and plucking (if applicable)
  - **Bath and Brush** (From $60 · Short coat breeds)
    - Full bath with skin-appropriate shampoo
    - Blow dry and deshedding brush-out
    - Nail cutting, filing, and sanitary shave
    - Ear cleaning

## Meet the Team Section
- **Heading:** Meet the Team
- **Team Members:**
  - **Dana — Owner**
    - **Bio:** Dana graduated from Animal Haven Dog Grooming School in 2017, specializing in precision grooming and hand stripping under master groomers. She joined Suds 'n' Scissors in 2022 and took ownership in 2025. Her approach combines behavioral understanding with expert styling to create a calm, personalized experience.
  - **Mana**
    - **Bio:** Mana graduated from Tokyo Communication Arts College in 2014, training in grooming, training, and veterinary assistance. After studying canine training in Australia, she joined Suds 'n' Scissors in 2017. Her gentle patience makes her exceptionally skilled with nervous dogs.
  - **Rachel**
    - **Bio:** Rachel has over 10 years of professional pet grooming experience. She trained at Western Dog Grooming School in 2014 and further refined her skills at Kim's Dog Grooming School in Seoul, Korea in 2017, bringing advanced techniques to every session.
  - **Nari Lee — Asian Style Stylist**
    - **Bio:** Nari completed her professional grooming training in Korea and is Fear Free Certified®. She specializes in low-stress, precision Asian Fusion styling, ensuring a calm, positive experience for every pet.
